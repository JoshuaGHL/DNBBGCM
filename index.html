Discussion Board

We have a discussion board for coaches and parents to share information and ask questions and get assistance from other coaches. It requires joining and to keep members from being spammed, Dwight Watt will approve requests to join list and also first message sent will pass through him for approval and then you will be able to post messages and not be reviewed. This is for protection of members of the discussion board.

Send an email to Dwight@dwightwatt.com if you have questions about the discussion board. To subscribe email Dwight at dwight@dwightwatt.com and request to subscribe and Dwight will subscribe you.

Yahoo Groups shut down and we no longer have the old page or list of subscribers from 1/2021 so if you were in old and want in new, you must subscribe to the new one.
