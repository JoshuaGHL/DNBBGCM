Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is the headquarters hotel? Do we have to stay there?

A: The Embassy Suites. You do not have to stay there but we have arranged excellent rates and the hotel is attached to the convention center where the match and ceremonies will be.

Q: Where will matches be shot?

A: The John Q Hammons Convention Center which is air conditioned.

Q: When should a state match be held by in order for a team to qualify to shoot in the DNBBGCM?

A: State matches should be held no later than March 30th.

Q: Is there a registration fee to shoot in the BB Gun match?

A: There is no registration fee this year for the BB gun match. There is a fee for late registrations.

Q: Can the same gun be used by different individuals or members of a team?

A: Yes they can, as long as the gun has been checked and approved for use before the competition.

Q: Can a team compete if there was no state match?

A: In BB Gun, the teams MUST have completed the Shooting Education course, and MUST have competed in a state qualifying match which can be hosted by an organization using current NRA competition rules and regulations. They must register with a qualifying team score.

Q: Where do I find information to have NRA sanction my state match?

A: http://nrahq.org/compete/dept-tourn-ops.asp
