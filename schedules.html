2022 Daisy National BB Gun & Air Rifle Championship Match

(All times & locations subject to change)

Wednesday June 29, 2022

6:00am to 12:00pm Range Set-Up & Pro-Shop Set-up

1:00pm to 4:00pm Early registration, Rogers Convention Center

1:00pm to 4:30pm Equipment Check, Rogers Convention Center, Dinner with Officials

Thursday Jun 30, 2022

7:00am to 4:00pm Registration, Rogers Convention Center

Friday July 1, 2022

8:00am to 5:00pm Pro Shop open, Rogers Convention Center

8:00am to 4:00pm Athletes Lounge, Ambassador A-B North Rotunda Hallway

8:00am to 10:00am Squadded practice, firing points 1A-52A. Range, Grand Ballroom 6-10 Rogers Convention Center Teams MUST practice on their assigned firing point.

8:00am to 9:00am Mandatory Orion scoring system clinic by Erik Anderson, Salon A & B North Rotunda Hallway. Only the registered coaches OR one team representative may attend. This is for Coaches that practice at 9:30 and 10:30am.

10:30am to 11:30am Mandatory Orion scoring system clinic by Erik Anderson, Salon A & B North Rotunda Hallway. Only the registered coaches OR one team representative may attend. This is for Coaches that practice at 8:00am.

9:30am to 11:30am Air Rifle Practice (Ambassador C & D)

10:30am to 12:30pm Squadded practice, firing points 1B-52B. Range, Grand Ballroom 6-10 Rogers Convention Center Teams MUST practice on their assigned firing point.

12:30pm Range closed for the remainder of the day

12:30pm to 3:00pm Painted Gun Contest in Range, Leave your painted rifles on the firing line with your completed forms. Guns will be judged and available to pick up after 3:00. The range will be locked at 12:45 pm and will not be open again till 3:00.

1:00pm to 2:00pm Orion Scoring Clinic with Erik Anderson, Salon A & B North Rotunda Hallway open to all interested parties Orion's Mobile App.

2:00pm to 3:00pm Test, Grand Ballroom 1-5, John Q Hammons Convention Center Champions will take test same time as teams. Coaches and teams arrive 10 minutes early to get cardboard backing and get your teams seated. Do NOT forget your team's answer sheets from your registration packets. If you do not retrieve the answer sheet(s) by the time the test begins, those test(s) will be scored as a ZERO.

4:00pm to 6:30pm Opening Ceremonies, Grand Ballroom 1-5, Rogers Convention Center Teams and the TWO registered coaches to be seated in the ballroom by 4:45pm. Staff will usher teams to designated assigned seating.

9:00am to 5:00pm Daisy Museum open
